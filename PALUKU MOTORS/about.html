About Paluku Motors

Who We Are

Founded in the heart of Kampala, Paluku Motors is a trusted and reliable car rental service provider located at Plot 2430, Banda. We specialize in offering clean, well-maintained vehicles for both individual and corporate clients across Uganda.

Our Mission

To provide affordable and dependable transportation solutions that empower travelers and enhance mobility across Uganda.

Our Vision

To become Uganda's most preferred car rental service by offering exceptional customer service and a diverse, high-quality vehicle fleet.

Meet the Team

Paluku Motors is led by a dedicated and professional team committed to excellence in customer service. From our CEO to our drivers and maintenance crew, we strive to ensure every customer has a smooth and safe experience on the road.

Why Uganda?

With its breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and growing tourism sector, Uganda offers limitless opportunities for travel and exploration. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Paluku Motors ensures you move conveniently and comfortably.
